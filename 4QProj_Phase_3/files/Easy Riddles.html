Easy Riddles


Let's start with some simple riddles. The rules are simple. Just pick the correct answer among the 4 choices. But remember, not everything is as it seems


1. What has to be broken to be used?

2. What is always in front of you but cannot be seen?

3. What goes up but never goes down?

4. A man who was outside in the rain without an umbrella or a hat didn't get a single hair on his head wet. How?

5. What can you keep after giving someone?
